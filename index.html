ABOUT RAYDIATE

OUR ORIGIN STORY

We are a passionate team of dreamers, engineers, and innovators who saw the need to close the energy gap, especially in marginalized communities.

We believe energy is not a luxury but a basic human right. That’s why we’re committed to delivering clean, reasonably priced, and reliable power to those who need it most.

At RayDiate, we're not just building energy solutions. We’re powering possibilities, transforming lives, and lighting the path to a brighter, more equitable future.

WHAT DO WE DO?

RayDiate provides accessible solar solutions, ranging from home kits to rooftop systems all designed to provide well founded power options to communities living in off-grid areas or those experiencing frequent power outages. We handle services such as installation and setup, maintenance support, training sessions as well as community solar education workshops. We ensure to not only provide light, but also lasting empowerment.

COMMUNITY IMPACT

Our Solar light is accessible to every person, even to marginalized communities. We are committed to ensure that people get their work done without fear of power failure, children can study even after dark, families can gather around in warmth and all communities can move forward. No one will be left behind.
